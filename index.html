01

Colour Palette

Four colours make up the entire Attention Culture palette. Hot Pink commands attention and is used sparingly. Black and White form the foundation of all communications. Grey provides a neutral supporting surface. Hover any swatch and click the copy icon to grab the hex code.

Hot Pink #EA124B Primary accent — CTAs, hero moments, highlights
Black #191919 Primary background — dark environments
Grey #EAEFEF Supporting surface — light environments, document sections
White #FFFFFF Light backgrounds, body text on dark surfaces

Use Hot Pink sparingly

Hot Pink is reserved for moments that demand attention — CTAs, key data points, highlight text, and key brand touchpoints. Overuse kills the impact.

Default to Black + White

Most brand communications live in Black and White. This creates a clean, high-contrast foundation that makes the Pink accent hit harder when it appears.

Grey for breathing room

Grey (#EAEFEF) is used as an alternative light surface — particularly in documents, presentations, and sections that need visual separation.

No gradients or tints

All colours are used flat and solid. No gradients, tints, or transparency overlays applied to brand colours. Opacity is reserved for text only.

Logo

Clear Space

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the bullet point (•) in the stacked mark. Nothing enters this zone.

Minimum Size

The stacked mark should never be reproduced smaller than 24px tall. The horizontal wordmark no smaller than 16px tall.

Never Do

Do not recolour. Do not stretch or distort. Do not place on photographic backgrounds. Do not recreate in any typeface other than Shrimp.

04

Tone of Voice

Attention Culture is a challenger agency with a conscience. Founded by people with 20+ years of agency experience across music, gaming, and FMCG, the brand voice is direct, purposeful, and culturally sharp — never corporate, never preachy. We speak with authority because we know this space, and we care about getting it right.

Direct

We say what we mean. No filler, no waffle. Every sentence earns its place. Short paragraphs, concrete claims, clear next steps. Readers are busy — respect that.

Purposeful

Everything we say connects back to the mission: conscious brand experiences. We don't just describe what we do — we articulate why it matters for brands, audiences, and culture.

Culturally Sharp

We are embedded in gaming, music, and entertainment culture. We reference it authentically, never as an outsider. Jargon is earned, not performed.

Confident, Not Arrogant

We hold strong positions on how brands should engage younger audiences. We share them clearly — but stay curious and collaborative, not dismissive.

We sound like this
  • Brands have a moral obligation to market consciously.
  • Gen Z aren't abandoning digital — they're rejecting algorithms.
  • Smaller communities. Deeper engagement. Better ROI.
  • Attention is human. It's sacred. Treat it that way.
  • Culture isn't a space to exploit — it's a space to protect.
Not like this
  • We leverage best-in-class synergies to optimise engagement metrics.
  • As a forward-thinking agency, we are passionate about our clients' success.
  • Touch base, circle back, move the needle, blue-sky thinking.
  • We're obsessed with disrupting the status quo! 🚀
  • It's all about creating value for stakeholders.
05

Usage

The brand system works across four environment types. Every colour combination is drawn from the four-colour palette. No environment should introduce colours, gradients, or surfaces outside these four approved backgrounds.

Dark Environment

Black background with white body copy and hot pink accents. The primary brand environment — used in decks, hero sections, reports, and digital products.

bg: #191919 / text: #FFFFFF / logo: white variant

Pink Environment

Reserved for high-impact brand moments — cover pages, key data callouts, CTAs. White logo and white text only. Never tinted or modified.

bg: #EA124B / text: #FFFFFF / logo: white variant

Light Environment

White background with black body copy. Used for document interiors, supporting slide layouts, and print materials. Hot Pink may be used sparingly as accent.

bg: #FFFFFF / text: #191919 / logo: black variant

Grey Environment

Grey background with black body copy. Used within documents to create visual hierarchy and section separation without switching to full white.

bg: #EAEFEF / text: #191919 / logo: black variant

The Conscious Framework™

The Conscious Framework™ is Attention Culture's proprietary operating system for conscious creativity. It is the foundation on which we execute our mission — ensuring everything we touch is done with intention, ethics, and emotional intelligence.

Our purpose
To create safe digital experiences for future generations.

47% of Gen Z and Gen Alpha battle with depression, hopelessness, and poor body image — and they spend half their time on smartphones. The solution is to craft digital experiences which positively impact the audience and leave a lasting impression. With us, brands have an opportunity to tell meaningful stories, grab attention, and remain culturally relevant — with a sense of responsibility for the impact they have on young minds.

Value 01

Conscious

Create something with the audience experience front of mind. Ask if we should, before how we do.

Value 02

Positive

Leave every audience better than you found them.

Value 03

Mindful

Create with care for who might see, hear, or feel it. Design for intended and unintended audiences alike.

Value 04

Impactful

Build things that move people and move the world forward.

Framework Pillars

The four pillars that structure how we execute the Framework across every project and client engagement.

01

Governance & Documentation

Formal policies, codes of conduct, and documented commitments that define how we operate — internally and with every client and collaborator.

02

Risk & Impact Assessment

Every brief includes a mental health impact lens. Creative concepts go through an empathy checkpoint. We measure impact not just in impressions, but in impression left on minds, moods, and memories.

03

Purpose Alignment

We only work with brands whose goals align with our values. Every engagement starts with purpose alignment — ensuring commercial objectives and conscious principles are not in conflict.

04

Performance & Transparency

ROI plus Return on Impact. We report on both. Campaigns are debriefed against commercial outcomes and social value outputs — and clients see both sets of results.

Social Media

LinkedIn is Attention Culture's primary social channel. Posts follow a consistent visual format — square carousel slides built within the brand system. Captions follow the tone of voice framework: direct, culturally sharp, and purposeful.

Post Format
1:1
Square

1080 × 1080px recommended. Carousel posts of 5–10 slides. Each slide is self-contained — readable without the one before it.

Profile Image
1:1
Circle

400 × 400px minimum. Use the stacked logo mark on a black or pink background. Never the horizontal wordmark — too small at this size.

Banner / Cover
~8:1
Wide

1584 × 396px. Use a dark or pink background with the horizontal wordmark, a brand statement in Neue Metana, or a combination of both.

Carousel Slide Anatomy

Every post in the series follows the same structural logic — a consistent header, a bold central statement, and a swipe prompt. This builds recognition across the series.

Slide structure
  • 1. Series header — small Menlo text top-left: "ATTENTION CULTURE / SERIES NAME / DATE"
  • 2. Hero statement — large Neue Metana Bold, ALL CAPS, fills majority of the slide
  • 3. Supporting copy — Menlo body text, supporting the headline stat or claim
  • 4. Swipe prompt — "→ Swipe to see where they went" in Menlo, bottom-aligned
Caption style
  • → Open with the key stat or provocation — no preamble
  • → 2–3 short paragraphs maximum. Each one earns its place.
  • → End with a direct question or CTA — invite discussion, not just views
  • → 3–5 focused hashtags at the end. Never mid-caption.
  • → No emojis unless part of a deliberate brand moment
Primary slide bg bg: #191919 — most slides
Cover & key stat slides bg: #EA124B — used sparingly
Data & source slides bg: #FFFFFF — supporting content

Boilerplate Copy

Standard agency descriptions for use across bios, directories, pitches, speaker profiles, and press enquiries. Use the shortest version that fits the context. Do not paraphrase — these are the approved wordings.

One line — for directories, social bios, speaker tags

A specialist independent agency connecting brands with audiences in gaming and music.

Two lines — for pitch decks, partner introductions

Attention Culture is a specialist independent agency connecting brands with audiences in gaming, music, esport, and sport. We create conscious brand experiences in entertainment — crafting activations that prioritise ROI, impact, and the wellbeing of younger audiences.

Full paragraph — for press, long bios, award entries

Attention Culture is a specialist independent agency connecting brands with younger audiences in gaming, music, esport, and sport. Our mission is simple: to create safe digital experiences for future generations. We craft conscious brand activations that prioritise commercial impact, audience wellbeing, and lasting cultural relevance — underpinned by our proprietary Conscious Framework. We create conscious brand experiences in entertainment.
